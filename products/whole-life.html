Whole Life Insurance

Whole life insurance is also known as permanent insurance. The level premiums paid build up cash value which can be used for:

  • Retirement
  • Savings
  • Financial Emergencies
  • Loans

Participating Life Insurance

A whole life insurance policy that pays annual dividends is called a participating whole life policy. These policies can build up cash value faster than regular whole life policies because the insurance carrier will pay an annual stipidend which can be used to:

  • Build up cash value
  • Pay part of the policy's cost
  • Purchase a term insurance bonus
